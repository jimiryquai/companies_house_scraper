# Product Mission (Lite)

Companies House Scraper is a lead generation system that helps Business Reset Group identify UK companies in distress by automatically extracting and enriching data from Companies House records to generate qualified leads for distressed company assistance services.

Companies House Scraper serves Business Reset Group by automatically monitoring "Active - Proposal to Strike Off" companies in real-time, extracting complete director information, and providing enriched lead data ready for CRM integration. Unlike manual research or generic business databases, it focuses exclusively on distress signals and provides immediate competitive advantage through real-time Companies House API integration and comprehensive director intelligence.
